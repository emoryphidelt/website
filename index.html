About Us

On Saturday, January 27, 2018, Phi Delta Theta officially recolonized the Georgia Beta Colony on the campus of Emory University in Atlanta, Georgia. Phi Delta Theta was originally founded at Emory University on June 15, 1871. The chapter maintained a presence and impact on Emory’s campus and the greater Atlanta area for 142 years.

Join Us

Phi Delta Theta was founded in 1848 at Miami University on the principles as friendship, sound learning and rectitude.
